Dallas Commercial Photo Studio

Bedding, furniture, and product photography

Welcome to Madigan Studios! We are based in Dallas, Texas and shoot both studio and location photography. We specialize in bedding and furniture photography as well as large and small product. We are a modern digital studio that has a wide range of photographic capabilities. From children's bedding to adult bedding, dining rooms to lamps, flatware to electronics. If you need digital retouching done, including color correcting, we are staffed to handle your requests. The studio includes 30,500 square feet with a full set building shop, a 24’ cyclorama, full kitchen, client areas, prop areas, pressing rooms, and merchandise storage areas. This allows us to offer quick turn-around times and competitive pricing. We use the latest digital equipment and computers and deliver superior quality to all of our clients. Take a look around the website to see some of the work we’ve done, and feel free to contact us for an estimate on your next project.
